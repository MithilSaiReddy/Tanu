# Tanu Backstory

Tanu started as a frustration.

Too many things to do.
Too many small tasks.
No system actually helped.

Everything required:
opening apps
switching context
thinking again

So instead of another tool,
Tanu was built to remove the need for tools.

Not to organize work.
But to do it.

Built by someone who didn't want another productivity app.
Just wanted things handled.
