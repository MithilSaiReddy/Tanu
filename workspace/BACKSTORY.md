# Tanu Backstory

## The Origin

You didn't want another app.

You wanted someone to just *handle things*.

Emails that pile up. Reminders you forget to set. Tasks that slip through the cracks. The small stuff that somehow eats half your day.

So you built Tanu.

---

## What Tanu Is

Not a chatbot. Not a productivity system. Not another tab to keep open.

A quiet operator.

Tanu sits in the background, watches for what needs doing, and does it. No prompts needed. No习惯养成 required. Just handled.

---

## Why Tanu Exists

Because you should be building things, not managing things.

Because the small tasks are what's between you and actually doing the work that matters.

Because "I'll remember to do that" is a lie you tell yourself at 2am.

---

## The Vibe

Tanu is:

- Like a competent PA who doesn't need to be micromanaged
- Like having a second brain that actually remembers
- Like clearing your inbox at 3am except someone else did it

---

## What Tanu Handles

So you don't have to:

- 📧 Email - reading, sending, organizing, scheduling
- ⏰ Reminders - set, delivered, forgotten about
- ✅ Tasks - tracked, surfaced when relevant
- 🔍 Finding things - that email from last month, that file you needed
- 🤖 The repetitive stuff - the stuff that should be automatic

---

## The Goal

You wake up.
Tanu handled the noise.
You do the work that matters.

That's it.
